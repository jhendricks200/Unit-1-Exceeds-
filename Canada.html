Travel Photography

Travel Photography is by far my favorite type of photography. I love to travel and exprience new places. I took this shot in Western Canada traveling from the town of Banff to the town of Jasper along the Ice Fields Parkway.

Shot Framing

Shot Framing as displayed in this photo is utilizing your environment to frame an image. That can be done by shooting through a tree branch and getting leaves in the shot. In this case I was in a cable car heading up a mountain. I utlized the window of the car to capture the town of banff in the Canadian Rockies.
